Price Analysis¶

As it can be seen from Statistical Data Exploratory, there are some outlier with the price variable. It has a min of 0 and max of 10,000. So let's get rid of the outliers.

In [11]:
price = airbnb[(airbnb['price'] > np.quantile(airbnb['price'], 0.25) - (1.5 * iqr(airbnb['price']))) & (airbnb['price'] < np.quantile(airbnb['price'], 0.75) + (1.5 * iqr(airbnb['price'])))]

X = price.drop(['price', 'host_id', 'id', 'name', 'host_name', 'last_review', 'latitude', 'longitude'], inplace=False, axis=1)
y = price['price']

lm.fit(X_train, y_train)
